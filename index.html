About Alvaro

Your Trusted Makhana Partner

At Alvaro, we specialize in sourcing and delivering plain makhana (foxnuts) of the highest purity. Whether you are a wholesaler, a retail brand, or an individual buyer, our makhana stands out for its crunch, consistency, and quality grading.


We've built strong relationships with farmers and developed rigorous quality control processes that ensure every batch meets our exacting standards.


Why Choose Alvaro?

  • Direct farm sourcing for freshness
  • Rigorous quality grading system
  • B2B and B2C focused service
  • Consistent supply chain reliability
